# Frequency of Use

The more often a user sees an animation, the shorter and subtler it should be.

- Remove animations or hover interactions altogether if they are seen tens or hundreds of times a day
- Instead of delighting users, repetitive animations quickly become annoying and make the interface feel slower
- A faster-spinning spinner makes the app seem to load faster, even though the load time is the same

## Rule of Thumb

## The Frequency Gate

Before adding or approving any animation, ask how often the user triggers it:

| Frequency | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|
| Rare (monthly) | Delightful, expressive motion welcome |
| Occasional (daily) | Subtle, fast motion |
| Frequent (100s/day) | No animation or instant transition |
| Keyboard-initiated | Never animate |

## Rule of Thumb

- Seen once per session → Can be more expressive
- Seen tens of times daily → Keep it shorter or remove it entirely
